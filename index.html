How this works

It is super simple. If you have recently traveled or lived somewhere, go to the "contribute" tab on the top/bottom right of each page. It will take you to a Google Form where you can submit information about your trip. Send me a text so that I know that you have updated this (this is a low-tech process ;)) and I will include your pin and info in the site

On the other side of things, you are planning a trip and want to know what someone who has lived there or recently been there recommends. Scan the map to find if the spot has been added. It will show you their suggestions but also will let you know who provided it, and give you a link to their facebook account. You have more questions? Reach out to them. A friend sourced travel advice one-stop-shop.

One day I think it would be cool if you could log in with your facebook account and have it be a more interactive system. People could "like" things but you wouldn't get annoying notifications that don't relate to you, because nobody's got time for that.

All that to say, if you know how to make that happen, let me know and we can make this a team effort!.
